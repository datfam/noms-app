Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

NOMS is built with a "Privacy First" mindset. Your data belongs to you.

1. Image Processing

All image processing, including background removal for your stickers, happens 100% on your device. We never upload your photos to any external servers.

2. Data Storage

Your food journal and spending data are stored locally on your iPhone and synced securely via your personal iCloud account.

3. Location Services

Location data is only used to tag your entries locally if you grant permission. We do not track or share your location.
